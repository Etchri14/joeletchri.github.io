Engineering Profile

I build mechanical and electromechanical systems with attention to requirements, risk, validation, and maintainability. My work connects CAD design, tolerance analysis, test planning, supplier communication, and manufacturing feedback into a single engineering workflow.

I am especially interested in motion and control applications: hydraulic power units, pneumatic fixtures, sensor integration, automation cells, and endurance testing. I document decisions clearly so designs can be reviewed, transferred, serviced, and improved.
